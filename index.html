Design and Analysis of a Drivetrain of an Electric Formula Student Vehicle

In this paper, we have discussed the theory and analysis of the drivetrain system of an electric Formula Student Vehicle that incorporated a student-developed system. The paper discusses the theory and basic design criteria of the drivetrain that is used to transmit the power and torque to the wheels and tyres. The main objective of the paper is to design a feasible, achievable vehicle with the budget we have to complete the vehicle in to compete in the Formula Bharat 2021 Competition. The Power to weight ratio has to be increased as much as possible with keeping the vehicle as light as possible without compromising driver's safety. All the designs were made by strictly following Formula Bharat 2021 Electric Vehicle specifications described in 2021 rulebook

DOI: 10.13140/RG.2.2.31022.95040

Design and Analysis of a Hydraulic Brake System of an Electric Formula Student Vehicle

Weight reduction is one of the prime concerns for a race vehicle; particularly electric vehicle. We aimed to design a braking system with the optimum size and feasibility while keeping the driver's safety in mind and the budget available. In this paper, the design choice of the braking mechanism has been discussed. Various calculation of braking force, braking torque and brake bias are presented. Likewise, the security of utilizing a self-planned brake plate is validated by estimations, calculations and thermal analysis.

DOI: 10.13140/RG.2.2.29295.25769

Effects due to use of nanoparticles in refrigerants

In the present study, the effects of nanoparticles on refrigerants have been briefly studied for research and literature review, several papers have been studied by four individual students as a part of the sessional examination to know the various effects including Lubricity and material compatibility, Heat pipe efficiency enhancement with refrigerant-nanoparticles mixtures, Nanoparticles enhance power reduction and Thermal Conductivity and Viscosity of Al2O3/water nanofluid.
